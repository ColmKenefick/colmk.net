Hypnotic Brass Ensemble

Post-Produced & mixed by Colm Kenefick.

Behind The Boards

The Hypnotic Brass Ensemble (HBE) are eight sons of the jazz trumpeter Phil Cohran, from the South Side of Chicago.

They have performed with Mos Def, Aquilla Sadalla, Phil Cohran, The Recipe, Nomadic Massive, Tony Allen, Wu Tang Clan, De La Soul, Prince, Femi Kuti, Gorillaz, and the B52's.

I was initially given one piece to mix down; once they heard that mix I was asked to complete the entire EP. This was the first collaboration in an ongoing working relationship with the HBE.
